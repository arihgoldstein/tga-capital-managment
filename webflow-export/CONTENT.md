## Intro Section

**Title:** Active Investment Management

**Body:** At TGA Capital Management, we believe in active portfolio management that responds to changing market conditions. Our managed account services provide you with professional oversight and strategic adjustments designed to optimize your investment performance while managing risk.

---

## Services Grid

### Individual Accounts
Personalized investment strategies for your taxable brokerage accounts. We work with you to develop a portfolio aligned with your goals, time horizon, and risk tolerance.

### Retirement Accounts
Expert management of your IRA, 401(k) rollover, or other retirement accounts. We focus on growth while protecting your nest egg as you approach retirement.

### Joint Accounts
Investment management for couples and families. We help you coordinate your financial goals and create a unified investment strategy.

### Trust Accounts
Professional management for trust assets. We work with your estate planning team to ensure investments align with trust objectives.

---

## Process Section

**Title:** Our Investment Process

### Step 1: Discovery
We begin with a comprehensive review of your financial situation, goals, risk tolerance, and time horizon.

### Step 2: Strategy Development
Based on our discovery session, we develop a customized investment strategy designed to meet your specific objectives.

### Step 3: Implementation
We construct your portfolio using a diversified mix of investments selected to execute your strategy.

### Step 4: Ongoing Management
We continuously monitor your portfolio, making adjustments as market conditions change and your life circumstances evolve.
